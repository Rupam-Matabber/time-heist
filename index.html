TRACKS

Web Dev

Web Development is the building and maintenance of websites; the work that happens behind the scenes to make a website look great, swift and efficient with a seamless user experience.

Web3

Web3 is an idea for a new iteration of the World Wide Web which incorporates concepts such as decentralization, blockchain, cryptocurrencies, (NFTs), and other token-based economics.

App Dev

App Development is the process of creating software applications. It includes user interface design, programming, testing and deployment of the application.

AI-ML

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning involves the usage of complex algorithms that automatically learn and refine the learning from a vast amount of data and data patterns.

IoT

IoT describes the network of physical objects that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the internet.

Rule Book

CRITERIA

Team Size :-  1 - 4
Who Can Participate :-  Anyone
About The Hackathon :-

Hackathon is basically a kind of contest where some problem statements are given with a certain duration and in that duration, you have to solve any one of the given problems- statements by building any project that can be any website or app or any IoT device.

Rules :-

-> All the Teams should be registered within the due date.

-> Every Team will be of 2 to 4 members.

-> Every team must use any version control system that can track your progress(e.g. GitHub, GitLab)

-> Every team has to document their code properly.

-> All teams must submit their project within the due date.

-> After submission, an interview will be arranged where you have to describe your project to all your team members.

-> The teams will be disqualified if the project is found copied or abusive to any other society/community.

PRIZES

Prize Money :-

Mind Webs Ventures shall provide the event with prize money amounting to Rs. 20,000/- for the winners of the hackathon as cheques/drafts of Rs. 10,000/-, Rs. 6,000/- and Rs. 4,000/- for the Winner, First and Second Runners up respectively.

Launch of MVP :-

Mind Webs Ventures shall help the winners of the hackathon to launch their Minimum Viable Product (MVP) in the market by providing them with guidance and resources as a fostered product under the Venture.

Fostering of Products :-

Mind Webs Ventures and its associates will foster two products, one for the Winner of the Event, and one special domain for Hardware and IoT Excellence Product (which can be from the top three or a separate category award all together). So, in total two Products to be pushed to MVP stage.
